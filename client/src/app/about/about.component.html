About Us

Poomies Bakery is a family-owned business that has been serving freshly baked goods to our community for over a decade. Our passion for baking and commitment to using the finest ingredients have made us a local favorite.

At Poomies Bakery, we believe in the magic of baking. From our mouthwatering custom cakes to our delightful sweet pastries, each creation is crafted with love and care. We aim to create delicious treats that bring joy and happiness to our customers' lives.
